Mega Millions includes a 2X–10X multiplier on every $5 play automatically — no add-on to choose.
10X only runs when the jackpot is $150M or less. Match 5 + Power Play pays a flat $2M. It never touches the jackpot and doesn't change your odds — just bigger non-jackpot payouts. Tell the clerk "with Power Play" at purchase; you can't add it after.
The Numbers Behind It ▼
Straight talk: every lottery draw is independent and random. Past results have zero effect on the next draw — a "hot" number and a "cold" number have identical odds. Hot / Overdue / Balanced just shape how the numbers feel, not your chances. This is for fun. Play responsibly.
